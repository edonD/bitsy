# Bitsy First Category Pack: AI Customer Support SaaS

## Category

AI customer support and helpdesk software for B2B SaaS teams.

## Why This Category

This is a strong first wedge because buyers ask clear recommendation questions:

- best customer support software
- best Zendesk alternatives
- Intercom vs Help Scout
- best AI chatbot for SaaS support
- helpdesk for small SaaS teams

The category also has obvious competitors and clear content fixes: comparison pages, alternative pages, proof pages, integration pages, support automation pages, and review-site presence.

## Best First Prospects

Do not start by selling to Zendesk, Intercom, or Salesforce.

Start with smaller challengers that care about being compared against those leaders.

Good prospect profile:

- 10-200 employees
- sells to SaaS, ecommerce, or support teams
- has a blog or resources section
- has comparison pages or should have them
- competes against Intercom, Zendesk, Freshdesk, or Help Scout
- has a founder, head of marketing, or content lead who is reachable

## What To Track Per Answer

For each AI answer, record:

- target mentioned: yes/no
- target position if mentioned
- competitors mentioned
- winning competitor
- answer sentiment toward target
- sources or citations if available
- exact raw answer
- missing reason, if obvious

## Common Gap Patterns

Use these labels in the report:

- Missing comparison page
- Weak category language
- Not enough third-party proof
- Competitor has stronger review-site presence
- Competitor has clearer pricing page
- Competitor has better use-case page
- Competitor has stronger migration story
- Competitor is cited by listicles or review sites
