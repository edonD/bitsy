# Bitsy First Sales Sprint

## The Immediate Goal

Do not launch broadly yet.

The goal for this sprint is simple:

> Get 3 serious replies from real AI customer support SaaS companies.

If the replies are strong, Bitsy has a real wedge.

If the replies are weak, fix the offer before building more product.

## The Wedge

Start with one category:

**AI customer support / helpdesk SaaS**

This category is a good first wedge because buyers compare many similar tools, the market is crowded, and companies care a lot about being recommended when buyers ask AI tools what to buy.

## The First 5 Prospects

Start with these five:

1. Fini
2. Tidio
3. Crisp
4. Help Scout
5. Gorgias

Do not start with 100 companies.

Five is enough to learn fast.

## What To Build First

Build one sample report.

The report should answer four questions:

1. Does the company appear when buyers ask AI tools for recommendations?
2. Which competitors appear instead?
3. What buyer questions are they losing?
4. What should they change first?

The report should be short.

Best format:

- 1 executive summary page
- 1 results table
- 1 competitor gap page
- 1 fix list page
- 1 rerun plan page

## The 10 Questions To Test

Use these questions first:

1. What is the best AI customer support software for SaaS companies?
2. What is the best Intercom alternative for AI customer support?
3. What is the best Zendesk alternative for a small SaaS team?
4. Which AI support tools can resolve tickets automatically?
5. What customer support platform is best for startups?
6. What is the best helpdesk with AI chatbots?
7. Which AI customer support tools are easiest to set up?
8. Which support tools work best for reducing ticket volume?
9. What are the best customer support platforms for B2B SaaS?
10. Which AI support tools are good for fast-growing software companies?

## What To Send

Use this message:

```text
I checked how AI tools answer buyer questions in your category.

You show up in some answers, but competitors are winning several high-intent questions.

I made a short report showing:
- where you appear
- who beats you
- what pages or content likely need to change
- what I would test next

Want me to send it?
```

## What Not To Say Yet

Avoid these claims:

- We can guarantee higher AI rankings.
- We know exactly how ChatGPT ranks brands.
- We retrain daily.
- This is causal attribution.
- This is a fully automated platform.

Say this instead:

> We measure how AI tools answer real buyer questions, find where competitors are winning, recommend the first fixes to ship, then rerun the same questions to see what changed.

## 48-Hour Checklist

Day 1:

- Choose one target company.
- Run the 10 buyer questions across ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini.
- Capture the answers.
- Mark whether the target appears, where it appears, and who wins.
- Write the top 3 competitor gaps.

Day 2:

- Turn the findings into one short PDF.
- Send it to five prospects.
- Track opens, replies, objections, and calls booked.

## Success Criteria

This sprint is working if:

- 3 people ask to see the report.
- 1 person asks how pricing works.
- 1 person agrees to a call.

This sprint is not working if:

- people say the problem is interesting but do not want the report.
- people only ask for a free dashboard login.
- people do not understand what changed after reading the message.

## The Offer

Start with a service-led offer:

**AI Visibility Fix List**

Includes:

- 10 buyer questions
- 3 AI tools
- competitor breakdown
- short fix list
- one rerun after a shipped change

Suggested starting price:

**$750 to $1,500 for the first report**

Raise the price only after prospects clearly understand the value and you can repeat the delivery process.

## The Next Decision

After the first 5 prospects, decide based on evidence:

- If people reply: sell the report manually.
- If people ask for proof: make the sample report stronger.
- If people do not understand: rewrite the message.
- If people want automation: build only the part they keep asking for.

The product should follow the sales conversation, not the other way around.
