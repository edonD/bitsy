# Bitsy First Outreach Campaign

## Target Category

AI customer support and helpdesk SaaS.

## Target Prospect

Sell to challenger brands, not the giant incumbents.

Good prospects:

- small or mid-market helpdesk companies
- AI support tools
- Intercom alternatives
- Zendesk alternatives
- support automation tools
- customer support chat tools

Target roles:

- founder
- head of marketing
- product marketing lead
- content lead
- SEO lead

## Prospect List Rules

Add a company if:

- it sells customer support software
- it has at least 3 obvious competitors
- it has a blog, resources, comparisons, or alternative pages
- it is not already the default market leader
- the website makes it clear who the buyer is

Skip a company if:

- it is too broad
- it has no clear category
- it has no public content to fix
- it only sells enterprise services
- it is too large to care about a small report

## Outreach Message 1

Subject if email:

`I checked if AI recommends [Company]`

Message:

> Hey `[Name]`, I’m building Bitsy.
>
> It checks whether ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini recommend your product when buyers ask category questions.
>
> I ran a small sample for `[Company]`. You showed up in `[x]` of 3 answers. `[Competitor]` appeared more often.
>
> Want me to send the 1-page version?

## Outreach Message 2

Send 2-3 days later.

> The useful part is not the score.
>
> It is the fix list: which page, claim, or source could help `[Company]` show up more often when buyers ask AI tools for support software.
>
> Happy to send the sample if useful.

## Outreach Message 3

Send 5-7 days later.

> Closing the loop here.
>
> The sample is small: 3 buyer questions, 3 AI tools, and the competitor that won each answer.
>
> If AI visibility is on your radar this quarter, I can send it over.

## Reply Handling

If they say yes:

> Great. I’ll send the 1-page teaser.
>
> If it is useful, the full report covers 10 buyer questions, competitor breakdown, raw answers, 3 recommended fixes, and one rerun after you ship a change.

If they ask pricing:

> The pilot version is $750. It includes the full report and one rerun after 14-30 days.

If they ask how it works:

> We run the exact buyer questions across ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini, record the answers, compare your brand against competitors, and turn the gaps into a short fix list. No black-box score.

If they ask whether it proves causality:

> No. It tracks before and after runs honestly. AI answers shift for many reasons. The value is seeing where you are missing, shipping one clear fix, and rerunning the same questions.

## Success Metrics

First 2 weeks:

- 100 prospects researched
- 30 messages sent
- 10 teaser reports created
- 5 conversations
- 1 paid pilot

If fewer than 3 replies after 30 messages:

- improve the teaser
- make the first line more specific
- show the competitor that beat them
- narrow the prospect list

If people reply but do not buy:

- lower friction with a $250 mini-report
- ask what would make it worth paying for
- make the fix list more concrete
