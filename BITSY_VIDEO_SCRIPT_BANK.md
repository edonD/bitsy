# Bitsy Video Script Bank

These scripts are built for simple screen-recorded product videos. Use real product screens, a fake demo brand, and plain captions.

Recommended demo setup:

- Demo brand: Acme Support
- Competitors: Intercom, Zendesk, Help Scout, Freshdesk
- Buyer question: "What are the best AI customer support tools for SaaS companies?"
- Product promise: see where AI recommends you, where competitors win, what to fix, and whether the fix worked.

Length: 90-120 seconds

Best for: website hero video, demo page, investor/customer intro

### Visuals

1. Show AI answer recommending competitors.
2. Show Bitsy setup screen.
3. Show results table.
4. Show competitor gap.
5. Show fix list.
6. Show before/after rerun.

### Voiceover

Your buyers are asking AI tools who to buy from.

They ask:

"What are the best AI customer support tools for SaaS companies?"

And before they ever visit your website, ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini may already be recommending your competitors.

Bitsy shows you what is happening.

Add your brand, your competitors, and the questions buyers actually ask.

Bitsy checks those questions across ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini.

You see where your brand appears, where it is missing, who wins each answer, and the raw AI response behind every result.

Then Bitsy turns the gaps into a fix list.

Maybe you need a comparison page.

Maybe your pricing page is missing proof.

Maybe competitors are being cited from review sites where you do not appear.

After you ship the fix, run the same questions again.

Now you can see whether your brand showed up more often.

Bitsy helps SaaS teams answer four questions:

Where do AI tools recommend us?

Where do competitors beat us?

What should we fix first?

Did the fix work?

Bitsy.

Know where AI recommends you. Know what to fix next.
